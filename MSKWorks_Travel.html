Travel


Ireland


A trip to Ireland in 2017 to celebrate my 50th birthday.

Started in Dublin for a day then to a rail tour for four nights/five days. Back to Dublin for a few days. While in Dublin, did a bus tour to Northern Ireland/Belfast for Giant's Causeway. Walking tour to Trinity University, the Books of Kell and Dublin Castle. Music Tour Pub crawl, lots of shopping...it was an amzing trip.



Europe


My employer closed down in 2004 so with some time off (thanks to a severance), I took a trip to Europe with my niece.

Started with our flight to London for a couple days so we could do Stonehenge and Bath. Joined the bus tour from London through Dover, over to Amsterdam, down through Germany and Switzerland to Italy then back up to France and eventually home.



Florida
